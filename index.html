Why travel to Sydney

Sydney is a great place to travel, there are alot of things to do there. From the United States it is a nice place to go in the December, January, and Febuary since our winter is their summer. Here are some of the attractions and thing to do when you get there.

  • Sydney Harbor as shown above can make a whole week of the trip
  • Sydney Opera house is beautiful
  • Darling Harbor for sights and entertainment
  • Bondi Beach about 20km from the harbor for beach day

Things to Consider

Plan well in advance to travel to Australia. There are many things to take into consideration and many things to get organized before you leave and when you get there. Here are some things you should get thinking about before you go.

  • What is the currency and where do I get it?
  • What transportation is available when I get there?
  • Should I stay in the heart of the city or further out?
  • What events are going on, farmers markets, shows, sporting events?
